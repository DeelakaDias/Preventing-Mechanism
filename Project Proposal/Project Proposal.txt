Introduction 

The apparel industry is one of the most demanding industries across the world. There is an uncountable number of sewing machines in a single plant. Most of the company's still checklist methods to store the records of sewing machine. For example, they are making checklists once the sewing machine is serviced. During a sewing machine breakdown, there might be certain rules that are obeyed by the technicians. Therefore, it is taking a considerable amount time to repair the machine back to its working status. Time is the most important factor to any company. When time wasted due to a sewing machine, it is a very critical situation for an apparel company, it might cause to lose a customer as well. Downtime causes loss of production, low revenue and low efficiency and many more. The apparel industry, as it is, does not have a system to identify as to why a machine undergoes a breakdown. Therefore, coming up with a solution to identify a breakdown prior will help to overcome these issues. I'm using an algorithm called Temporal Fusion Transformer to predict the breakdown of sewing machine accurately. This is a time series forecasting AI model.
